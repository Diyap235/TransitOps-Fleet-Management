🎨 DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

  Gradients:
    • Primary: Blue-600 → Cyan-500
    • Hover: Blue-700 → Cyan-600
    • Bright: Blue-400 → Cyan-400
    • Table Headers: Blue → Cyan gradient

  Glassmorphism:
    • Cards: rgba(255,255,255,0.85) + blur(10px)
    • Inputs: rgba(255,255,255,0.8) + blur(4px)
    • Topbar: rgba(255,255,255,0.85) + blur(10px)
    • Dark theme: rgba(30,36,51,0.8) + blur(12px)

  Shadows:
    • Cards: Layered near + distance shadows
    • Buttons: Soft + glow effect
    • Inputs: Inset + outer focus ring

  Animations:
    • Easing: 0.18s cubic-bezier(0.4,0,0.2,1)
    • Hover Lift: -2px to -3px
    • Login Blobs: 6-8s float animation

💡 CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

Change Primary Color:
  1. Find #2563EB (blue) → Replace with your blue
  2. Find #0EA5E9 (cyan) → Replace with your cyan
  3. Update dark theme equivalents

Adjust Glassmorphism:
  • Opacity: rgba(255,255,255,0.85) → Higher = more transparent
  • Blur: blur(10px) → Increase/decrease as needed

Modify Shadows:
  • Reduce rgba(37,99,235,0.08) for softer shadows
  • Increase for more dramatic depth
